About the League

Building Stronger Communities, One Cast at a Time

The Youth Fishing League (YFL) was founded on the belief that outdoor experiences, especially fishing, have the power to bring families together, strengthen communities, and instill a sense of responsibility for nature in future generations. We are more than just a fishing organization; we are a movement dedicated to empowering youth, building stronger communities, and preserving the natural beauty of our local waterways.

Our Mission

YFL’s mission is to foster a love for the outdoors through recreational fishing while teaching valuable lessons in environmental stewardship, community collaboration, and personal growth. Through our events and educational programs, we aim to inspire youth to appreciate the environment, cultivate lifelong skills, and contribute to the well-being of their communities.

What We Do

Each YFL event is designed to be an engaging, family-friendly experience centered around fishing. From teaching the basics of casting to identifying different fish species, our activities are hands-on and tailored to participants of all ages. But it’s not just about catching fish; it’s about fostering curiosity, respect for nature, and a sense of shared responsibility. In addition to fishing, we incorporate educational lessons about environmental stewardship, such as the importance of protecting our local waterways and understanding the ecosystems around us. With the support of local partners and volunteers, YFL events also feature lessons on community history and the cultural significance of fishing to our region.

Our Impact

In August 2024, we hosted a community fishing day at Clopper Lake in Seneca Creek State Park, bringing together 30 parents and children for a memorable day of laughter, learning, and outdoor fun. Many first-time fishers caught their first fish, including some impressively large Bluegills.

This event was made possible through a collaborative effort, with incredible support from Seneca Creek volunteers. We provided rods, bait, and water, ensuring everyone could fully enjoy the experience without barriers. The joy on the children’s faces as they reeled in their catches was unforgettable.

Inspired by this success, we aim to host regular community fishing events through the Youth Fishing League, promoting outdoor engagement, family bonding, and environmental stewardship.

Our Values

​Inclusivity: We welcome families of all sizes and backgrounds, ensuring that everyone can participate regardless of ability, experience, or financial situation.

Stewardship: Teaching environmental responsibility is at the heart of everything we do. We believe in educating youth and families about the importance of protecting our waterways and preserving our natural resources.

Community: YFL is deeply rooted in the communities we serve. Our events are made possible through local partnerships, sponsorships, and volunteer efforts, creating a network of support that extends beyond the fishing pond.
