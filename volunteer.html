Event Volunteer

  • Responsibilities:
    • Teach basic fishing skills to kids and parents (casting, rod setup, bait)
    • Assist with event setup/breakdown and equipment distribution
    • Manage registration and check-in
    • Create a welcoming environment for first-time fishing families
    • Document events through photos and participant stories
  • Requirements:
    • Attend training sessions on fishing techniques and environmental education
    • No prior experience required
  • Location: In-person (DC metropolitan area)
  • Time Commitment: Flexible, typically 5-10 hours per event

Site & Partner Coordinator

  • Responsibilities:
    • Create photo/video content of event locations for social media
    • Conduct in-person interviews with partner organizations
    • Document potential fishing spots and event spaces
    • Support partnership development through site visits
    • Research local environmental regulations and fishing requirements
  • Requirements:
    • Complete required training on fishing techniques and environmental education
    • No prior experience required
  • Location: In-person (DC metropolitan area)
  • Time Commitment: Flexible, typically 5-10 hours per event

Digital Engagement Volunteer

  • Responsibilities:
    • Produce weekly newsletters with fishing tips, youth spotlights, and recipes
    • Manage social media accounts and community engagement
    • Create event graphics and promotional materials
    • Research partnership and grant opportunities
    • Organize participant data and analyze event feedback
  • Requirements:
    • Familiarity with Canva, Google Workspace, and social media platforms
    • Optional portfolio of prior design or social media work
  • Location: Remote
  • Time Commitment: 5-10 hours per week
