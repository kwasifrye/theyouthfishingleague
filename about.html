ABOUT THE LEAGUE

The Youth Fishing League (YFL) was founded on the belief that outdoor experiences, especially fishing, have the power to bring families together, strengthen communities, and instill a sense of responsibility for nature in future generations. We are more than just a fishing organization; we are a movement dedicated to empowering youth, building stronger communities, and preserving the natural beauty of our local waterways.

What We Do

Each YFL event is designed to be an engaging, family-friendly experience centered around fishing. From teaching the basics of casting to identifying different fish species, our activities are hands-on and tailored to participants of all ages. But it’s not just about catching fish; it’s about fostering curiosity, respect for nature, and a sense of shared responsibility.

In addition to fishing, we incorporate educational lessons about environmental stewardship, such as the importance of protecting our local waterways and understanding the ecosystems around us. With the support of local partners and volunteers, YFL events also feature lessons on community history and the cultural significance of fishing to our region.
